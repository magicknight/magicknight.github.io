A compact, modular online CT system designed for seamless integration into production lines. It delivers high-speed, high-precision inspection with a target spatial resolution better than 50µm, supported by our core AI-driven reconstruction algorithms.

Intelligent Sparse 3D Reconstruction

This is our core innovation. Traditional CT is slow because it needs vast amounts of projection data. Our sparse reconstruction technology acts like a skilled detective, piecing together the full picture from minimal clues. It uses advanced AI to "in-fill" a complete, precise 3D structure from far less data, achieving a revolutionary 2-5 second scan time.
